Frequently Asked Questions
 
What is Magento Development Agency?

An organisation that specialises in offering Magento-related services, such as website development, customisation, maintenance, and support, is known as a Magento development agency. These companies frequently employ a group of talented programmers, designers, and e-commerce specialists who are experts at using the Magento platform to build dependable, customised online stores for companies.

Does Magento require coding?

While Magento offers a user-friendly interface for basic tasks, more advanced customization and development may require coding skills. A Magento developer should be familiar with technologies such as PHP, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to tailor the platform to specific business needs, integrate extensions, and create a unique and feature-rich online store.

What language is Magento?

PHP is the main server-side programming language used by Magento. Magento integrates HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for front-end development and uses MySQL as its database management system.

What is Magento customization?

Customization of magento involves tailoring Magento e-commerce platform to meet specific business requirements. This includes designing a unique user interface, implementing custom features, integrating third-party extensions, and optimizing the performance of the online store.

Is Magento free to use?

Magento is available in two editions:

 

Magento Open Source - free to use, and users can download, install, and modify the software according to their needs

 

Magento Commerce - Magento Commerce is a premium, feature-rich edition that comes with additional functionalities and support.
